Exhibition Postsers  It is a design for the exhibition—“I don't like it.”,featuring artworks that had once been disliked by the public, rejected by art critics, or hated by the artists themselves.  Wikibook  I designed the content of a full Wikipedia article “ Religion in China.” Using calm and organized design aesthetics, I would like to communicate the profound cultural heritage of this topic.  Manifesto Poster   For this project, I designed a poster for the Apple's manifesto--“Here's to the crazy ones” and applied the design to Shanghai Youth Art Fair 2020. I explored unconventional compositions to capture the unique spirit and boldness of this manifesto.  Mantra Merch   I designed for the mantra “All is well. I am loved.” Using pure typographic design in black and white, I would like to explore the process from confusion to certainty.  Posters Collection  I designed a series of posters based on the ideas of influential scholars of Graphic Design. Then I made a zine titled “Aha? Design!” to showcase this serious with the article MASSIMO VIGNELLI VS. ED BENGUIAT (SORT OF).
